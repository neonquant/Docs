# NeonQuant User Guide

This guide explains how to use the NeonQuant app from a user perspective.

## What the app does

NeonQuant gives you a simple market outlook for supported cryptocurrencies using live market data and in-app forecasting tools. It is designed to help you quickly review:

- recent price movement,
- short-term hourly projections,
- a next-day estimate, and
- an optional 7-day outlook.

> **Important:** The app is for informational use only and does **not** provide financial advice.

## Supported assets

You can switch between these assets inside the app:

- BTC
- ETH
- SOL
- BNB
- XRP
- ADA
- DOGE
- AVAX
- LINK

## Getting started

1. Open the app.
2. On the launch screen, read the disclaimer.
3. Tap **Begin Analysis**.
4. Wait while the app pulls market data and prepares your forecast.
5. Once loading finishes, the main dashboard will appear.

If the app cannot load data, tap **Retry** and try again.

## Reading the dashboard

After the app loads, you will see several sections.

### 1. Asset header

At the top of the screen, the app shows:

- the current asset symbol,
- the asset name, and
- the **Last close** price.

Use this area to confirm which coin you are viewing.

### 2. Price Trend Overview

This chart gives you a quick view of recent market movement plus the app's next-day estimate.

Use it to:

- spot the recent direction of the asset,
- compare current price action with the projected move, and
- get a fast visual summary before reading the detailed forecast sections.

### 3. Short-Term Outlook (Hourly)

This section is for near-term movement.

It shows:

- an **Estimated price** based on the number of hours you choose,
- a **Chance of moving up in the next hour**, and
- an hourly forecast chart.

#### How to use the hourly slider

1. Find the **How far ahead** slider.
2. Drag it to choose a forecast window from **1 hour to 24 hours**.
3. Watch the estimate and chart update to match your selected time range.

Use shorter ranges when you want a quick near-term read, and longer ranges when you want to see the app's full 24-hour view.

### 4. Tomorrow's Outlook (24h)

This section summarizes the next-day forecast.

It includes:

- the **Estimated price by tomorrow**, and
- the **Chance price is higher tomorrow**.

This is the fastest place to check the app's overall 24-hour outlook.

### 5. Extended Outlook (Next 7 Days)

The 7-day forecast does **not** load automatically.

To use it:

1. Scroll to the **Extended Outlook** section.
2. Tap **Load 7-day forecast**.
3. Wait while the app builds the weekly forecast.
4. Review the resulting chart, **Day 1 estimate**, and **Day 7 forecast**.

If the app cannot build the weekly forecast, try again after a moment.

## Changing the asset

To switch to another cryptocurrency:

1. Tap **Change** in the top bar.
2. Select the asset you want.
3. Wait for the app to refresh the dashboard.

The app will reload the forecasts for the newly selected asset.

## Refreshing the current forecast

After the app has loaded, a circular **Refresh** button appears.

Use it when you want to:

- pull a fresh outlook for the current asset,
- update the charts, or
- re-run the prediction after market conditions change.

Tap the refresh button and wait for the loading overlay to finish.

## Understanding the probability indicators

The app uses percentage-style probability displays to describe direction.

As a general guide:

- around **50%** means the outlook is relatively neutral,
- **above 50%** suggests an upward lean,
- **below 50%** suggests a downward lean.

These values are estimates, not guarantees.

## If the app cannot fetch data

Sometimes market data may be temporarily unavailable.

If that happens:

1. Wait a minute.
2. Check your internet connection.
3. Tap **Retry** or run the forecast again.

If a market data provider is temporarily unavailable, the app may ask you to try again shortly.

## Best way to use the app

A simple workflow is:

1. Tap **Begin Analysis**.
2. Review **Price Trend Overview**.
3. Check **Tomorrow's Outlook** for the main 24-hour estimate.
4. Use the **Short-Term Outlook** slider for a shorter time window.
5. Tap **Load 7-day forecast** if you want a broader weekly view.
6. Use **Change** to compare another supported asset.
7. Tap **Refresh** whenever you want updated results.

## Reminder

NeonQuant is meant to help you review market information quickly, but it should not be the only input in a financial decision. Always do your own research before acting on any forecast.
