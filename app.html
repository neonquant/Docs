    Overview

    What the app does

    NeonQuant gives you a simple market outlook for supported cryptocurrencies using live market data and in-app forecasting tools. It is built to help you quickly review recent price movement, short-term hourly projections, a next-day estimate, and an optional 7-day outlook.

    • Recent price movement
    • Short-term hourly projections
    • A next-day estimate
    • An optional 7-day outlook

    Start here

    Getting started

    1. 1
      Open the app.
    2. 2
      On the launch screen, read the disclaimer.
    3. 3
      Tap Begin Analysis.
    4. 4
      Wait while the app pulls market data and prepares your forecast.
    5. 5
      Once loading finishes, the main dashboard will appear.

    If the app cannot load data, tap Retry and try again.

    Main dashboard

    Reading the dashboard

    After the app loads, you will see several sections designed to give you a fast view of current conditions and forecast direction.

    1. Asset header

    At the top of the screen, the app shows the current asset symbol, the asset name, and the Last close price. Use this area to confirm which coin you are viewing.

    2. Price Trend Overview

    This chart gives you a quick view of recent market movement plus the app's next-day estimate.

    • Spot the recent direction of the asset
    • Compare current price action with the projected move
    • Get a fast visual summary before reading the detailed forecast sections

    3. Short-Term Outlook (Hourly)

    This section is for near-term movement. It shows an Estimated price based on the number of hours you choose, a Chance of moving up in the next hour, and an hourly forecast chart.

    How to use the hourly slider
    1. 1
      Find the How far ahead slider.
    2. 2
      Drag it to choose a forecast window from 1 hour to 24 hours.
    3. 3
      Watch the estimate and chart update to match your selected time range.

    Use shorter ranges when you want a quick near-term read, and longer ranges when you want to see the app's full 24-hour view.

    4. Tomorrow's Outlook (24h)

    This section summarizes the next-day forecast. It includes the Estimated price by tomorrow and the Chance price is higher tomorrow. This is the fastest place to check the app's overall 24-hour outlook.

    5. Extended Outlook (Next 7 Days)

    The 7-day forecast does not load automatically.

    1. 1
      Scroll to the Extended Outlook section.
    2. 2
      Tap Load 7-day forecast.
    3. 3
      Wait while the app builds the weekly forecast.
    4. 4
      Review the resulting chart, Day 1 estimate, and Day 7 forecast.

    If the app cannot build the weekly forecast, try again after a moment.

    Asset switching

    Changing the asset

    1. 1
      Tap Change in the top bar.
    2. 2
      Select the asset you want.
    3. 3
      Wait for the app to refresh the dashboard.

    The app will reload the forecasts for the newly selected asset.

    Forecast refresh

    Refreshing the current forecast

    After the app has loaded, a circular Refresh button appears.

    • Pull a fresh outlook for the current asset
    • Update the charts
    • Re-run the prediction after market conditions change

    Tap the refresh button and wait for the loading overlay to finish.

    Interpretation

    Understanding the probability indicators

    The app uses percentage-style probability displays to describe direction.

    • Around 50% means the outlook is relatively neutral
    • Above 50% suggests an upward lean
    • Below 50% suggests a downward lean

    These values are estimates, not guarantees.

    Troubleshooting

    If the app cannot fetch data

    Sometimes market data may be temporarily unavailable.

    1. 1
      Wait a minute.
    2. 2
      Check your internet connection.
    3. 3
      Tap Retry or run the forecast again.

    If a market data provider is temporarily unavailable, the app may ask you to try again shortly.

    Suggested workflow

    Best way to use the app

    1. 1
      Tap Begin Analysis.
    2. 2
      Review Price Trend Overview.
    3. 3
      Check Tomorrow's Outlook for the main 24-hour estimate.
    4. 4
      Use the Short-Term Outlook slider for a shorter time window.
    5. 5
      Tap Load 7-day forecast if you want a broader weekly view.
    6. 6
      Use Change to compare another supported asset.
    7. 7
      Tap Refresh whenever you want updated results.

    Final note

    Reminder

    NeonQuant is meant to help you review market information quickly, but it should not be the only input in a financial decision. Always do your own research before acting on any forecast.
